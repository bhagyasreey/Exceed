Train Announcement System

Instructions

Enter Announcement Text:

  • Use this box to type announcements related to train departures or arrivals.
  • Example 1: "The train number 12589, Howrah Express, is arriving at Platform 3 in 5 minutes."
  • Example 2: "The train number 12657, Chennai Express, is now ready to depart from Platform 2."

Make Announcement:

  • After typing the announcement, click the Make Announcement button to generate and play the voice announcement over the station system.



Voice Transcription Tool

Instructions

Follow these steps to use the Speech-to-Text Logger efficiently:

  1. Click on the Start Voice Recognition button.
  2. Speak your message or announcement clearly.
  3. The system will automatically convert your voice into text in real time.
  4. Review, edit, and save the transcript for future reference.



Digitization of Handwritten Station Notes

Instructions

  1. Click the Upload Image button to select a handwritten text image from your device.
  2. Use a clear, high-quality image for best results.
  3. Click the Convert to Text button after uploading.
  4. Check the extracted text and make any edits in the text box.
  5. Save the text for future reference or use it as needed.
